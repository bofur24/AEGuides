Valinor is the location were players start their adventer in the game of Ashen Empires.

Starting Town Quest Guides


Silvst is the home of Humans. Their city lies on Krythan Island. Their bonus orbs are in Intelligence.


Krog is the homeland of the Orcs. Their city lies in the blackSwamp in the center of the Mainlands. Their bonus orbs are in Strength.


Whisperdale is the home of the Night Elves. Their city lies in Darkwood Forest on the west end of the Mainlands. Their bonus orbs are in Dexterity.
