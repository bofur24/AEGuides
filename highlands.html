Hero of Dawat

Highland Dawat
Difficulty Skills
Apprentice Combat
NPC Quest Start Reward

Player: Yes! I will quell their evil.
Shargar: Good! Good news. You set good example. must stay near Dawat, not wander off to easy Cultists in other lands.
QUEST RECEIVED: Hero of Dawat, After speaking to Shargar in Dawat, you vowed to kill 50 Cultists!


Head out to the northwest of town to find the Cultist. You will need to kill them with in the coordinates for them to cout as a kill for Shargar. You will be updated as you kill them, QUEST UPDATE: You have killed 50 Cultists! When you have killed all 50 you will be updated to return to Shargar.
QUEST UPDATE: Hero of Dawat, Return to Shargar in Dawat to collect your reward!

Shargar: Good! You made dent in evil...maybe no new followers, hmm? reward for you now! come back, work more. Shargar pay well.
QUEST UPDATE: Hero of Dawat, You cleared the cultists away from the area

  • Gold Received: 500
  • 27,500 Experience Pool experience may very

Shargar: I go bragging about you, but learn bad news. Cultist not hurting yet. Have big bosses, Cult Leaders, they wear purple robes... Cut off the head, body falls. All warrior know that. Go seek leaders out.. They near Cultists

Head out to the northwest of town to find the Cultist Leaders. You will be updated as you kill them, QUEST UPDATE: You have killed 75 Cult Leaders for Shargar! When you have killed all 75 you will be updated to return to Shargar.
QUEST UPDATE: Hero of Dawat, Return to Shargar in Dawat to collect your reward!

Shargar: Good, good. You show these young Orc what hero is. Here is reward! Come back. More work! Live in dark times.

  • Gold Received: 750
  • 41,250 Experience Pool experience may very

Shargar: *The Orc Glares* I happy to see you, but now even more problems to northwest. When cut a tree throns grow up in the sun. Cultist cut back. Now Thieves set up shop in abandoned warehouse below Abbey. You cannot stop now. Must stop thieves. Kill them.
QUEST UPDATE: Hero of Dawat, Kill 100 Thieves for Shargar in the warehouse south of the Abbey.


Head out to the northwest of town to find the warehouse with the Thieves. You will be updated as you kill them, QUEST UPDATE: You have killed 100 Thieves for Shargar! When you have killed all 100 you will be updated to return to Shargar.
QUEST UPDATE: Hero of Dawat, Return to Shargar in Dawat to collect your reward!

Shargar: Good work. You brave warrior. Here is reward. Shargar always pay...

  • Gold Received: 1,000
  • 13,750 Experience Pool experience may very

QUEST UPDATE: Hero of Dawat, Take down Andreev the Quick, the leader of the Thieves.


Head out to the northwest of town to find the warehouse with Andreev the Quick. Kill him then return to Shargar for your reward.
QUEST UPDATE: Hero of Dawat, You've defeated Andreev the Quick. Return to Shargar in Dawat to collect your reward!

Shargar: Dawat feels safer! You true friend of Dawat. Saved many tossing around nights. Now even little Orcs playing pretend hero games. Wear badge proudly in our town...

Quest Log Location
Highland Dawat
Difficulty Skills
Apprentice Combat
NPC Quest Start Reward
Kudah

Kudah: Kudah welcomes you friend! Kudah friend of earth. Are you friend of earth?
Player: I am a friend of the earth.
Kudah: Kudah happy to hear that Kudah work with the earth, and earth work with Kudah. The earth grant Kudah special gifts of knowledge. Kudah seek to pass on gifts to friends. Kudah want be shaman, but Kudah need to practice hard. Help Kudah practice knowledge and Kudah pass on gift.
Player: I will gladly help you Kudah.
Kudah: Kudah happy again! Kudah work on special garment, make us one with earth. Kudah need help gathering special pieces to make garment, make us one with earth. Kudah need help gathering special pieces to make garment. First Kudah need poison sac from Fire Ant. Fire Ants live near mountain, southwest of Kudah. Bring Kudah 1 Poison Sac and be careful. Kudah need Sac intact.
QUEST RECEIVED: Friend of the Earth, Kudah of Dawat wants you to collect a Poison Sac from Fire Ants.


Head southwest next to the mountain to find the Fire Ants. You will be updated as you kill them. You were unable to keep the Poison Sac intact. Keep looking! When you have succed in keep the Poison Sac intact you will be updated to return to Kudah.
QUEST UPDATE: Friend of the Earth, You found a Poison Sac for Kudah. Return to Dawat.

Kudah: Good work! Now Kudah need 3 Skeleton Toes. Skeletons wander by mountains near Krog. Bring 3 Toes to Kudah.
QUEST RECEIVED: Friend of the Earth, Hunt Skeletons that wander the mountains north of Krog to collect 3 Skeleton Toes for Kudah.


Head west to the mountain range north of Krog to find the Skeletons. You will be updated as you kill them. You found a Skeleton Toe for Kudah! When you have succed in finding all three you will be updated to return to Kudah.
QUEST UPDATE: Friend of the Earth, You have retrieved 3 Skeleton Toes. Return to Kudah in Dawat.

Kudah: Kudah halfway there. Skeleton Toes prove useful on garment; Kudah grind toes and make fine powder to treat garment. Make garment strong! Now Kudah need you hunt Warg have very large pelt, and Kudah need very large pelt. Warg found all near village, so shouldn't be hard.
QUEST RECEIVED: Friend of the Earth, Kill Wargs and collect 1 Very Large Pelt for Kudah.
