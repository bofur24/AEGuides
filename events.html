Thanksgiving

Skills
Combat
Monster Reward
Wild Turkery (lvl 30)
Gobble Wobble (lvl 80)

The event is to kill Wild Turkies that spawn around the Mainlands. Killing them has a chance of droppping one of the four totems. There is also a boss turkey named Gobble Wobble that will spawn as well. There will be a message that gives you a hint to were the boss will spawn.


Once you found and killed Gobble Wobble he will drop one of the four totems, and a few random items.
There will also be another system message to let all know that Gobble Wobble has been slain.
